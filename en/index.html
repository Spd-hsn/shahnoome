HOW IT WORKS

One New Step into Mythology Every Week

Each week, children explore one story: they listen, play, create, and at the end of the course, they get to “talk” with Ferdowsi.

Story of the Week

Each week features an interactive audio story chosen to suit the child’s age, interests, and emotional development.

Listen and Play

Children take part in games and interactive activities throughout the story, becoming the hero of the adventure.

Create and Imagine

After each story, children complete a creative activity that helps the ideas stay with them.

A Conversation with Ferdowsi

At the end of each course, “Mr. Ferdowsi” responds to the children’s questions and curiosities.

WHY SHAHNOOME?

Five Reasons to Join the Journey

Shahnoome is more than storytelling. It is a safe and joyful way for children to connect with their Persian roots.

Made for Children

Stories are selected according to the child’s age and developmental needs.

Start from the Beginning

No previous knowledge of the Shahnameh is needed.

Weekly Interaction

Creative missions and games after every story reinforce learning and help children feel part of a heroic world.

Questions Answered by Ferdowsi

Children’s questions are answered at the end of the course.

Fully Flexible

Designed around family schedules, so children can take part whenever it suits them best.
